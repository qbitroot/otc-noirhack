pSymm

Private & Symmetric OTC Trading

The Problem

  • Current Trading Options are Suboptimal:
  • DEX: Complete exposure of trading activity
  • CEX: Loss of fund custody and control
  • Public orderbooks (CLOB/AMM):
    • No privacy, front-running risks
  • Existing OTC solutions
    • High costs and barriers to entry
    • Limited access for smaller traders

Our Solution: pSymm

  • Trade privately off-chain
  • Collateral secured on Aztec L2
  • Dispute resolution mechanism
  • P2P architecture enabling full private OTC
  • Trade any derivatives: perps, stocks, FX, ETFs

Solution: Case Study

Traditional DEX Scenario:

  • A whale wants to open a $300M+ position
  • Using a DEX exposes their position to everyone
  • Traders try to liquidate the position

With pSymm:

  • Same whale opens $300M+ position privately
  • With pSymm, executes trade off-chain
  • Only trader and counterparty see the trade
